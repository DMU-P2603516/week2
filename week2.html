WHAT WE LOVE ABOUT CATS

There are dozens of reasons to love a cat. They are cute felines who keep you company with their purr and fur. Not to mention their pretty eyes that reflect the luminance of the moon.

Here are list of things most people like about cats.

  1. They are cheap to feed;
  2. They can be left alone without supervision;
  3. They don't need to run outside;
  4. They are cute and they define it;
  5. They always land of their feet when in danger

They are cheap to feed

Perhaps you're on a budget but still wish to have a pet next to you. Well, cats are easy to mantain as the nutrients they require to live are really cheap.

They can be left alone

You don't always have the time to be home. You might have a busy routine and staying home is not an option most of the times. If that's the case for you, then having a cat is a recommended option as they can be left without supervision, unlike other animals.

They don't need to run outside

Cats don't require a routinely stroll in the park. Why? Because they themselves don't enjoy it. They'd rather be indoors either napping or exploring the house. However, if they require an adventure in the outside world, they don't necessarily need your help, as they can leave without supervision and return without a scratch.
